The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5 is an innovative foldable smartphone featuring a 7.6-inch main Dynamic AMOLED display and a 6.2-inch cover display, both with a 120Hz refresh rate for smooth visuals. It is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor with 12GB of RAM and storage options of up to 1TB. The camera system includes a 50MP main camera, a 12MP ultra-wide, and a 10MP telephoto lens, supporting 8K video.
